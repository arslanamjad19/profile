Hi, I'm Arslan Amjad|

I am currently a final-year Masters student at Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS), advised by Prof. Abubakr Muhammad and Prof. Hassan Jaleel. I'm passionate about Robotics and AI and want to work on solving non-trivial problems in these areas, especially, Agritech, Mobility and Healthcare.
